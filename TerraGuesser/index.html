Capital

Think you know cities? Narrow down the search for the world's major administrative centers.

How it works:

There is a secret location and its your mission to guess it in the fewest possible guesses.
Enter a city/country into the input field.
As a hint, you will get the distance between your guess and the target.
You can use this information to narrow down your next guess, or even triangulate the location!
